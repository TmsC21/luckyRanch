History of Lucky Ranch

Founded in 1952, Lucky Ranch has a rich history of horse racing and family traditions. Originally a small family-owned farm, it has grown into a place where history meets modern-day adventures. Over the decades, Lucky Ranch has won numerous local and national horse racing competitions.

Today, the ranch remains a testament to hard work and passion for nature and animals.

About Lucky Ranch

Lucky Ranch spans over 200 acres of lush, green landscapes. Our dedicated team has worked tirelessly to create a sanctuary for animals and an escape for visitors. Notable achievements include winning five regional horse racing championships and opening our doors to thousands of happy guests each year.

We pride ourselves on preserving traditions while providing a modern ranch experience.

Our Animals

Lucky Ranch is home to 11 magnificent horses, each with their unique personality and history. In addition to the horses, our mini-zoo features a variety of animals including goats, chickens, and rabbits, creating a perfect family-friendly experience for visitors.

Our animals are lovingly cared for and always ready to meet new friends!

Western City

Step back in time and explore our authentic Western City. Designed to immerse you in the charm of the Old West, this attraction features saloons, blacksmith shops, and even a sheriff’s office. Visitors can participate in cowboy-themed activities and enjoy reenactments.

Western City is a must-visit for anyone looking for a unique adventure!

The Western Bar

Our Western Bar is a throwback to the saloons of the past. With rustic decor and a warm atmosphere, it’s the perfect place to relax after a day at the ranch. Enjoy handcrafted drinks and delicious snacks while soaking in the Western ambiance.

Come for the drinks, stay for the experience!
